About us

Live Vibe is built on a simple but powerful belief: food should always be fresh. We proudly stand against stale cakes and snacks, and our motto is "Fresh Cake, Pastry & Snacks." Every product we serve is prepared with care, using natural fresh fruit extracts and high-quality ingredients to deliver authentic flavor, freshness, and superior taste.

At Live Vibe, we bring a unique and exciting concept to the bakery experience. Our live cake preparation allows customers to see the creativity and craftsmanship behind every cake and pastry. This visual experience creates transparency and trust, while making every visit enjoyable and memorable. We believe that customers deserve not only delicious desserts but also the joy of seeing their treats come to life.

Our passion for freshness drives everything we do - from selecting ingredients to designing beautiful cakes and pastries for every celebration. Whether it is a birthday, anniversary, special event, or a simple sweet craving, Live Vibe aims to make every moment more delightful with fresh flavors and vibrant presentation.

As we grow, our vision is to expand Live Vibe through a strong franchise network. We support our partners with training, operational guidance, and brand support so they can build successful businesses while maintaining the same freshness, quality, and customer experience that define our brand.

At Live Vibe, we are not just baking cakes - we are creating fresh experiences, joyful celebrations, and a vibrant culture of quality and trust in every cake, pastry, and snack we serve.
